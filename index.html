YouVidPlay is a planned app so it is not available yet. It will release at 2026.

Stay tuned for updates!

Features gonna have:

  • Video Playback: Watch videos seamlessly.
  • Search Functionality: Find your favorite videos easily.
  • Playlists: Create and manage your own playlists.
  • Customize your appearance: Choose themes and UI.
  • Offline Viewing: Download videos to watch offline.
  • Social Sharing: Share videos with friends.
  • User Channels: Create and manage your channel.
  • Notifications: Get updates on new videos and features.
  • Monetization: Earn money through memberships and subscriptions.
  • Ad-Free: Enjoy an ad-free experience without having a premium subscription.
  • AI-Powered: Ask Munetios AI to get answers like summarize videos, Help making a video, and more.
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Sync your preferences and watch history across devices.

At 2026. Do more with YouVidPlay.
